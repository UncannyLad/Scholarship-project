Akwa Ibom State Government Bursary Details:

The Akwa Ibom State Government is excited to announce the launch of the new and
improved Student Bursary and Support Grant Portal, a dedicated online platform designed
to simplify the application process for students applying for the yearly bursary payment.

This provides a gateway where every student from Akwa Ibom State studying in a public
(non-private) tertiary institution in Nigeria can apply for the yearly student bursary.

Akwa Ibom State Bursary 2025 Requirements:

To be considered for the Akwa Ibom State Bursary, you must:

  • be registered, full-time indigent but intelligent students in Federal or State Universities;
  • be at least in their 2nd year of study;
  • have a minimum of 3.50 Cumulative Grade Points Average (CGPA) on a 5.00 scale;
  • produce testimonials from your institution;
  • present certificate of origin from their respective Local Government Council in Akwa Ibom
    State;
  • NOT be more than Twenty-five (25) years of age at the time of application;
  • All applicants will be expected to state the financial status / occupation / means of livelihood
    of their Parents, after fulfilling the academic requirement;
  • All beneficiaries will be expected to submit academic results at the end of every session to
    determine their continued eligibility for the scholarship based on their CGPA, which will be
    verified by the Ministry of Education / State Scholarships Board;

Akwa Ibom State Bursary 2025 Duration and Reward

Qualified students will receive N20,000, while those studying professional courses like
Engineering, Medicine, Law, Agriculture, and Education (leading to qualified teacher status)
will receive N30,000.

Required Documents for Akwa Ibom State Government Bursary

To apply, candidates must be ready to submit the following documents;

  • Valid Student Registration Number
  • Valid Student ID Card
  • Valid Admission Letter
  • Valid Certificate of Origin
  • Valid School Fee Receipt
  • Be able to complete our digital identity checks
  • Valid NIN
